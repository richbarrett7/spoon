When even is spoon?

Spoon is a premium, award winning cereal brand that makes breakfast better using the best ingredients for your health and for the health of the planet. Spoon was put on earth to challenge the sugar-saturated, mass produced uninspiring sameness of breakfast.

Why Spoon?

What we put into our cereal is not just the best. It’s the actual best...

Our Great Taste Award-Winning granola has been carefully prepared in small batches with all natural, plant-based ingredients, which means a fresher granola and less waste.

We create our cereals with the same purpose we’d use if we were making them for our families at home...

Full of nutrient dense plant-based ingredients, mindful amounts of sugar and packed full of fibre.

And at the same time, we believe we are responsible for doing our bit for people and planet.

We are a business that gives back, donating 1% of our sales to local charities who are working to have a meaningful impact on our food systems and our environment and aligning with our own impact goals.
