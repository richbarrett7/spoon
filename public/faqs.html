General

Our Granolas

Which granola is best for a low-sugar diet?
We keep sugar to a minimum in all our cereals, but The Low Sugar One is your best bet. It contains just 3.3g of sugar per 100g and is high in fibre, made with wholegrain jumbo oats—a healthier carb choice than many processed cereals.
Do you offer gluten-free cereals?
Yes! Some of our granolas are certified gluten-free, produced in BRC-accredited facilities. Look for the gluten-free label on our packs.
Are your cereals vegan?
Absolutely! All Spoon Cereals products are Vegan Society certified.
Are your cereals organic?
Not all of them, but we use organic ingredients whenever possible. For example, our new porridge is made with Organic Oats. We're committed to sustainability and a more circular food system, prioritizing high-quality, responsibly sourced ingredients.
What type of sugars do you use?
We use natural sweeteners like maple syrup and date syrup. In The Low Sugar One, we use chicory fibre, a naturally occurring soluble dietary fibre from chicory root.
Is your packaging recyclable?
Yes! Our packaging is 100% recyclable. It consists of an outer cardboard box and an inner recyclable bag. We continuously explore ways to improve our sustainability efforts while ensuring our packaging remains eco-friendly.
